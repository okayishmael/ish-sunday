About Me

Hey there! I'm Ishmael, a Freelance Graphic Designer based in the lovely Washington D.C. Metropolitan Area.

My passion for art and creativity inspired me to pursue a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a focus on Graphic Design. Turning ideas into visuals allows me to experience my supterpower. Every design I create is a chnace to inspire, connect and leave a mark that matters.

What I Do

In a nutshell, I'm a print, digital and motion graphic designer specializing in visually communicating complex concepts in a clear and engaging manner.
